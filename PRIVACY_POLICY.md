# Privacy Policy for Namify Renamer

Namify Renamer is a offline file utility tool. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
Namify Renamer **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data. All file renaming operations are performed locally on your device.

## Internet Access
The application may use internet access only for:
1. Checking for application updates via GitHub/Microsoft Store.
2. Opening specified external links (like documentation or support) in your default web browser upon your initiation.

## Third-Party Access
Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

## Contact
If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the official support channels.
